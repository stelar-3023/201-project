About Us

We are a team of passionate kitesurfers who love to teach others how to ride the waves. Our experienced instructors will guide you through every step of the way, ensuring that you have a safe and enjoyable experience.

Our Mission

Our mission is to spread the joy of kitesurfing to as many people as possible. We believe that kitesurfing is not just a sport, but a way of life. By teaching others how to kitesurf, we hope to inspire a new generation of riders who share our passion for the ocean and the wind.

Our Instructors

All of our instructors are certified by the International Kiteboarding Organization (IKO) and have years of experience teaching kitesurfing. They are passionate about sharing their knowledge and helping students reach their full potential.

Gear

We use the latest gear from NP and Cabrinha. The expert instructors on our staff personally verify the safety of all the equipment prior to use. We also offer a variety of deals on new and used gear.
