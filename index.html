Lessons

Kite Control

A three-hour kiteboarding lesson covering the basics of kiteboarding. This lesson is the first step toward accelerating your learning curve and staying safe.

Get Up

The next three-hour kiteboarding lesson allows you to experience the true power of the kite in the water. This is the next step in order to get up and stand up! Skills you will learn:

  • Activating the safety quick release Re-launching the kite in the water
  • Controlling the kite with one hand while hooked into the harness Board
  • Recovery without using a board leash Performing self-rescues
  • Body dragging upwind
  • Water Start and riding short distance in both directions

Learn to Ride

This three-hour lesson emphasizes board control and riding. By the end of this lesson you will be standing up and on your way to being able to ride upwind. Skills you will learn:

  • Generating steady power with the kite
  • Proper body positioning and edge control
  • Board control
  • Riding in both directions
  • Appropriate gear selection for wind conditions
